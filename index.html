A Peek into My World

I graduated high school knowing that I wanted to make a difference in this world. I also graduated high school knowing that I will make a difference in this world. Nothing is going to get in my way. I mean, who doesn't want to feel like they have a meaningful impact?

But how does one make this difference?

I have chosen to take the path of education to make my difference. I want to learn and grow and become a better individual who can use this knowledge to better the world and further progress. I push myself each day to broaden my knowledge and gain more experiences. This goes beyond the classroom because a lot of important life lessons are not found in academic courses.

My life is about living and enjoying life too

Don't think that I only seek experiences to try to learn everything I can. Don't get me wrong, I value learning very much but I also find great importance in living life and enjoying every moment. What is life without living?
